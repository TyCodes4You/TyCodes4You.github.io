Why we like VHS

It's just still one of the best formats. There is a certain feeling you get from the warm glow of the analog image. The fallouts from the tape bring on nostalgia. The RGB colors take you to another world. And best of all, VHS is forever.

Why It's Better

  • Certain movies are still only available on VHS
  • VHS tapes are easy to rewind and start again
  • While not invincible, VHS tapes cannot be easily broken or scratched
