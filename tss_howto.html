Ease of Use

One of hte best benefits of the VHS is the ease of use. No ridiculous menus, no time-wasting, no nonsense. Just put the tape in, and off you go!

It's important to remember that VHS tapes are not indestructable. They also do require a bit of care to maintain their vintage nature.

Please be responsible. Do not:

  • Leave tapes in the sun
  • Pull tape out of case
  • Smash tapes with hammer
  • Give tapes away (They aren't yours)
